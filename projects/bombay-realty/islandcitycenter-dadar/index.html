Overview

Welcome to Island City Center spread across acres of lush open green spaces. 59 & 65 floors superstructure, One ICC & Two ICC, stand tall in the heart of Mumbai. Experience a more evolved way of living at ICC where the largest-in-class apartments are planned to bring your world closer to you.

Life at Island City Center is a state of luxurious well-being, cocooned in beauty, comfort and privilege. You live in a residence that exudes world-class style and splendour, offering spectacular views of the Arabian Sea & Bandra-Worli Sea Link. Your every wish and comfort is anticipated and provided for, on a scale grander than you could have imagined. With over 45 lifestyle amenities of International standards such as multi-sport courts, gym, landscaped garden, round the clock security – you have it all, right at your doorstep at Island City Center.

about us

a wadia group enterprise

Bombay Realty is a Wadia Group Enterprise that envisions to transform and redefine the Mumbai skyline with two prime developments, The Island City Center (ICC) located at Dadar (E) and The Wadia International Center (WIC) at Worli. With the ICC, nestled in lush greenery, built using world-class construction technology; Bombay Realty is set to introduce the world to the future epicenter of Mumbai-ushering in a whole new way of life. Not just an address.

For over 282 years, the Wadia Group has prided itself on its heritage of excellence and spirit of entrepreneurship. The coming together of these values, along with its unprecedented vision, has resulted in the birth of many successful ventures such as Bombay Burmah, Bombay Dyeing, Britannia and GoAir. The Wadia Group has accomplished landmark projects in real estate, be it residential, commercial or retail across India. In the recent past, The Wadia Group has delivered ‘SPRINGS’, a 40-storeyed luxury residential tower in Dadar (E) and C2 Wadia International Center, a state-of-the-art office building at Worli which serves as the headquarters of Axis Bank.
